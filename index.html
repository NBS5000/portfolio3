ABOUT ME

Having spent over a decade working in the technical areas of insurance, such as fraud investigation, litigation, complaints, and other fun parts of it, an opportunity arose for me to switch to something I had long held a passion and interest for.....coding.

Whilst living in Brasil, I studied web development through TAFE distance learning. Upon returning to Australia, I completed further courses, culminating in two TAFE diplomas, Software Development, and Web Development.

Once my courses were complete, I entered the development community, where I spent time volunteering with the Black Dog Institute, rebuilding and delivering their intranet. After this, I spent a number of years working with the FileMaker platform. Despite becoming an accomplished developer with this, I decided to leave and re-hone my skills with the University of Sydney Full Stack Bootcamp.
